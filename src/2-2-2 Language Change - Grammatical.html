Case inflection

What is case?

Case indicates the relationship of a constituent with other constituents in the sentence.

nominative case

He chased me.

She chased me.

The student chased me.

I am inevitable.

accusative case

I saw him.

The assassin killed them.

She kissed me.

I spoke to the student.

genitive case

Her dog chased him.

He chased my dog.

The floor is yours.

Kelly's name is Kelly.

dative case

He gave me a dirty look.

He passed her the book.

My teacher threw a chalk at me.

Let's give a toast to the newlyweds.

instrumental case

He tripped me with a stick.

She travelled here by ship.
